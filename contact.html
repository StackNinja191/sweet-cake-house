Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend placing orders at least 3-5 days in advance for standard cakes. For custom designs and wedding cakes, please order 2-3 weeks ahead.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery may be available for certain cake types, depending on our schedule. Please call us to check availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and all major debit/credit cards. Full payment is required at the time of order confirmation.
