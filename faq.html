Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How far in advance should I place my order? +
We recommend placing orders at least 3-5 days in advance for standard cakes. For custom designs and wedding cakes, please order 2-3 weeks ahead to ensure we can create exactly what you want.
Do you offer same-day delivery? +
Same-day delivery may be available for certain standard cakes, depending on our current schedule. Please call us at +92 316 0608032 to check availability.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, and all major debit/credit cards. Full payment is required at the time of order confirmation for custom cakes.
Can I customize any cake? +
Yes! We specialize in custom designs. You can choose the flavor, size, colors, decorations, and theme. Fill out our custom order form or contact us directly to discuss your vision.
Do you cater to dietary restrictions? +
Yes, we can accommodate various dietary needs including eggless, sugar-free, and nut-free options. Please mention your requirements when placing your order.
What is your delivery area? +
We deliver across Karachi. Delivery charges vary based on location. Contact us for specific delivery rates to your area.
How should I store my cake? +
Most of our cakes should be refrigerated and consumed within 2-3 days for best freshness. Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before serving for optimal taste.
Can I cancel or modify my order? +
Modifications can be made up to 48 hours before delivery. Cancellations made less than 48 hours in advance may incur charges as ingredients may already be purchased.
Do you offer cake tasting? +
Yes, for wedding and large custom orders, we offer cake tasting sessions by appointment. Please contact us to schedule a tasting.
What sizes do your cakes come in? +
We offer various sizes: Small (1 lb, 6-8 servings), Medium (2 lbs, 10-15 servings), Large (3 lbs, 20-25 servings), and Extra Large (4+ lbs, 30+ servings). Custom sizes available.
